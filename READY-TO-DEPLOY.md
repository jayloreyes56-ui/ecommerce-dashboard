## What You Need to Do (Manual)

### Go to Render and Deploy

**Why manual?** Render requires you to manually trigger deployment from their dashboard.

**Steps:**
1. Open https://dashboard.render.com
2. Find or create `ecommerce-dashboard` service
3. Click "Manual Deploy" or "Create Web Service"
4. Wait 3-5 minutes
5. Get your public URL

**Detailed instructions:** See `DEPLOY-NGAYON.md` (in Tagalog)
